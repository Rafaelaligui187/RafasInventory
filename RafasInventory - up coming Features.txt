### **Pages & Features**

1. **Login Page**

   * User login
   * Basic role distinction (Admin / Staff optional)

2. **Dashboard Page**

   * Overview:

     * Total products
     * Total stock quantity
     * Recent stock activity
     * Low stock alerts (basic visual, like a red warning)

3. **Products Page**

   * Add new products
   * Edit/delete products
   * Product details:

     * Name
     * SKU / Barcode
     * Category
     * Quantity
     * (Optional: product image)
   * Basic search (by name or SKU)

4. **Stock History Page**

   * Record stock in/out
   * Show simple history table (date, product, quantity, type of action)

5. **Reports Page**

   * Export current inventory to CSV or Excel
